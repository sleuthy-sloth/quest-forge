# Lore Bible: The Chronicles of Embervale

## The World
Embervale is a realm of flickering light surrounded by an encroaching void known as **The Hollow**. The balance of the world is maintained by the **Embershards**, magical crystalline fragments that resonate with the "True Deeds" of its inhabitants.

## Core Concepts
- **True Deeds**: Real-world responsibilities (chores, learning, discipline) that fuel the Emberlight.
- **The Hollow**: A corruption that feeds on neglect, disorder, and ignorance. It manifests as procedural bosses that haunt the edges of the realm.
- **Hearthhold**: The central sanctuary where families gather. It is the only place completely safe from the Hollow.

## The Five Classes
1. **Blazewarden**: Warriors who protect the light with physical discipline and courage.
2. **Lorescribe**: Masters of history and word, using knowledge to banish the shadows.
3. **Thornwood**: Guardians of nature who understand the living pulse of Embervale.
4. **Stormcaller**: Channelers of logic and science, wielding the fundamental forces of the world.
5. **Shadowveil**: Agitators of the void who walk the edge of the Hollow to better understand it.

## The Nine Faculty Trials
The Academy of Emberlight is home to nine masters, each guarding a discipline:
- **Math Arena**: Mastered by the Architect of Numbers.
- **Word Forge**: Guarded by the Scribe of Echoes.
- **Science Labyrinth**: Protected by the Alchemist of Truth.
- (And six other disciplines: History, Reading, Logic, Vocabulary, Life Skills, General Knowledge)

## Historical Arcs
- **Arc 1 (Weeks 1-4)**: The Awakening — The first encounter with the Hollow's scouts in the Ironspine Mountains.
- **Arc 2 (Weeks 5-8)**: The Overgrowth — Corruption spreads to the Heartwood Forest.
- **Arc 3 (Weeks 9-12)**: The Frost — The Hollow freezes the northern reaches.
